How to buy - DeFi Large Cap Momentum

We created this onboarding guide for people who are the first time users of crypto assets and Web3 wallet. It is provided for educational purpose.

Please choose one of the two options to start.

Easy

For new beginner, we recommend this option as it uses mobile app which is much more user friendly and you can buy stablecoin directly from the app. It requires less efforts.

Cost Effective

We recommend this option if you plan to deposit USD > $5000 as it charges less processing and trading fees. Ideally, you might want to try the Easy option with small amount first to get familiar with Web3 wallet and Web Dapp.

 easy

Step 1

Install Trust Wallet Mobile App

We recommend Trust Wallet because it is secure, decentralized and easy to use. It is also WalletConnect compatible which make it easy to connect to any Web Dapp in the later step.

Install Trust Wallet app and follow the onboarding instructions to create a new wallet.

Step 2

Buy stablecoin USDC

You would need USDC to deposit to the pool. You can buy USDC with fiat on-ramp provider in the app.

In the Trust Wallet app, open Wallet tab, tap Buy and enter USDC to buy stablecoin USDC token. Enter USD $ amount you want to get. You could choose MoonPay fiat on-ramp provider or any other providers your country support. Make sure you understand the processing fee before you proceed. You would be asked for ID verification before it is executed.

Step 3

Deposit stablecoin USDC to portfolio pool

DeFi Large Cap Momentum portfolio is deployed to Enzyme Finance decentralized asset management platform. To buy, deposit USDC in the pool. 

Enzyme provides Web Dapp that is WalletConnect compatible. You can connect Trust Wallet to it.

Open Enzyme Finance Web Dapp

Make sure the pool contract address you are interacting with is 0x4dc157c5acd2d149adc482d09b3c06b802b8f51c.

Click on top right "Connect Wallet", choose WalletConnect. A QR Code would be displayed. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app, go to Settings -> WalletConnect. Use the camera to scan the QR code showing on the Web Dapp. It will connect your Trust Wallet to Enzyme. This allows you to make smart contract transactions with Enzyme. 

You can now deposit USDC to the pool by clicking on "Deposit". Follow the instructions there. It involves 2 transactions: approving the token and executing the deposit. It will take a while for the transactions to settle. There is gas fee involved when executing any transactions in Ethereum.If the gas price is too high, you may want to wait until it is low to deposit. Check the current gas price at Gas Now. A confirmation popup will be shown once it is executed successfully.

To check your investment portfolio, go to the top menu "For Depositor" -> "My Deposits".

Step 1

Sign up in Binance.com

Sign up an account in Binance.com using our referral link with 10% offer trading fee. Follow the instructions to complete the full ID verification in order to increase the buying limit.

Step 2

Deposit your local currency

Now you can transfer your local currency from your bank account or credit cards. The most cost effective way to deposit is bank transfer but it usually takes time depending on your country. Deposit via Credit / Debit Card has extra fees. Some countries may have Third Party Payment options. Check the fees and weight up your preference between waiting time and fees.

Step 3

Buy stablecoin USDC

You will need stablecoin USDC in order to buy "DeFi Large Cap Momentum". From the top menu of Binance, Open Trade -> Classic, an order book trading platform with different pairs are shown.

The path to get USDC is to swap your local currency to USDT, then swap USDT to USDC. For example if your local currency is AUD, from right side of the webpage, enter AUD/USDT and click on it. At the bottom, enter the maximum amount of AUD you want to sell and click Sell AUD. When the transaction is executed successfully, you will have USDT. 

Next is to swap USDT to USDC. Similarly, enter USDC/USDT at the right side in the pair list search and buy USDC with all the USDT you got. 

Step 4

We recommend Trust Wallet because it is secure, decentralized and easy to use. It is also WalletConnect compatible which make it easy to connect to any Web Dapp in the later step.

Install Trust Wallet app and follow the onboarding instructions to create a new wallet.

Step 5

Withdraw USDC from Binance.com to Trust Wallet

From the Binance.com top menu, choose Wallet -> Overview. Click "Withdraw" and choose sUSD. You will need to add your Trust Wallet public address to the Address Management first. To get your public address, open Trust Wallet app. Goto Wallet tab, tap Receive and choose USDC. Copy the address and enter it in the Binance Address Management. If USDC is not in the list, go back to the Wallet tab, tap top right button and enter USDC to enable the token. 

You can now proceed with the USDC withdrawal. USDC is ERC20 token. Make sure you choose Ethereum blockchain network and double check the wallet address before confirming withdrawal. It will take a while for the transaction to complete. Once it's completed, check your Trust Wallet to make sure USDC is in.
