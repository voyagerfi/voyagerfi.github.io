Step 1

Install Trust Wallet Mobile App

We recommend Trust Wallet because it is secure, decentralized and easy to use. It is also WalletConnect compatible which make it easy to connect to any Web Dapp in the later step.

Install Trust Wallet app and follow the onboarding instructions to create a new wallet.

Step 2

Buy stablecoin sUSD

You would need sUSD to deposit to the pool. You can buy USDC first with fiat on-ramp provider provided in the app first then swap it to sUSD in decentralized exchange.

In the Trust Wallet app, open Wallet tab, tap Buy and enter USDC to buy stablecoin USDC token. Enter USD $ amount you want to get. You could choose MoonPay fiat on-ramp provider or any other providers your country support. Make sure you understand the processing fee before you proceed. You would be asked for ID verification before it is executed.

To get sUSD, you can swap USDC to sUSD in 1inch decentralized exchange with competitive price. 

You can connect to 1inch Web Dapp through WallConnect supported by Trust Wallet. Open 1inch Web Dapp, click on top right "Connect Wallet", choose Ethereum and WalletConnect. A QR Code would be displayed. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app, go to Settings -> WalletConnect. Use the camera to scan the QR code showing on the Web Dapp. It will connect your Trust Wallet to 1inch. This allows you to make smart contract transactions with 1inch.

You can now swap USDC to sUSD. It involves 2 transactions: approving the token and executing the swap. It will take a while for the transactions to settle. There is gas fee involved when executing any transactions in Ethereum. A confirmation popup will be shown once it is executed successfully.

Once it is completed, you can find it in your Trust Wallet. If it is not shown, tap on the top right icon in Wallet tab. Search for sUSD and enable it.

Step 3

Deposit stablecoin sUSD to portfolio pool

Large Crypto TrendMax portfolio is deployed to dHedge decentralized asset management platform. To buy, deposit sUSD in the pool. 

dHedge provides Web Dapp that is WalletConnect compatible. You can connect Trust Wallet to it.

Open dHedge Web Dapp

Make sure the pool contract address you are interacting with is 0x175db26fa692d44cece9b0c3d55764acb7c71349.

Click on top right "Connect Wallet", choose WalletConnect. A QR Code would be displayed. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app, go to Settings -> WalletConnect. Use the camera to scan the QR code showing on the Web Dapp. It will connect your Trust Wallet to dHedge. This allows you to make smart contract transactions with dHedge. 

You can now deposit sUSD to the pool by clicking on "Invest". Follow the instructions there. It involves 2 transactions: approving the token and executing the deposit. It will take a while for the transactions to settle. There is gas fee involved when executing any transactions in Ethereum.If the gas price is too high, you may want to wait until it is low to deposit. Check the current gas price at Gas Now. A confirmation popup will be shown once it is executed successfully.

To check your investment portfolio, go to "My Investment".

Step 1

Sign up in Binance.com

Sign up an account in Binance.com using our referral link with 10% offer trading fee. Follow the instructions to complete the full ID verification in order to increase the buying limit.

Step 2

Deposit your local currency

Now you can transfer your local currency from your bank account or credit cards. The most cost effective way to deposit is bank transfer but it usually takes time depending on your country. Deposit via Credit / Debit Card has extra fees. Some countries may have Third Party Payment options. Check the fees and weight up your preference between waiting time and fees.

Step 3

Buy stablecoin sUSD

You will need stablecoin sUSD in order to buy "Crypto Large Cap TrendMax". From the top menu of Binance, Open Trade -> Classic, an order book trading platform with different pairs are shown.

The path to get sUSD is to swap your local currency to USDT, then swap USDT to sUSD. For example if your local currency is AUD, from right side of the webpage, enter AUD/USDT and click on it. At the bottom, enter the maximum amount of AUD you want to sell and click Sell AUD. When the transaction is executed successfully, you will have USDT. 

Next is to swap USDT to sUSD. Similarly, enter SUSD/USDT at the right side in the pair list search and buy sUSD with all the USDT you got.

Step 5

Withdraw sUSD from Binance.com to Trust Wallet

From the Binance.com top menu, choose Wallet -> Overview. Click "Withdraw" and choose sUSD. You will need to add your Trust Wallet public address to the Address Management first. To get your public address, open Trust Wallet app. Goto Wallet tab, tap Receive and choose Synth sUSD. Copy the address and enter it in the Binance Address Management. If sUSD is not in the list, go back to the Wallet tab, tap top right button and enter sUSD to enable the token. 

You can now proceed with the sUSD withdrawal. sUSD is ERC20 token. Make sure you choose Ethereum blockchain network and double check the wallet address before confirming withdrawal. It will take a while for the transaction to complete. Once it's completed, check your Trust Wallet to make sure sUSD is in.
