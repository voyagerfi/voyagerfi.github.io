Step 1

Install Trust Wallet Mobile App

We recommend Trust Wallet because it is secure, decentralized and easy to use. It is also WalletConnect compatible which make it easy to connect to any Web Dapp in the later step.

Install Trust Wallet app and follow the onboarding instructions to create a new wallet.

Step 2

Buy stablecoin token

In the Trust Wallet app, open Wallet tab, tap Buy and enter USDC to buy stablecoin USDC token. Enter USD $ amount you want to get. You could choose MoonPay fiat on-ramp provider or any other providers your country support. Make sure you understand the processing fee before you proceed. You would be asked for ID verification before it is executed.

For DeFi Large Cap Momentum, you can continue to Step 3.

For Crypto Large Cap TrendMax, you would need sUSD to deposit to the pool. To get sUSD, you can swap USDC to sUSD in 1inch decentralized exchange with competitive price. 

You can connect to 1inch Web Dapp through WallConnect supported by Trust Wallet. Open 1inch Web Dapp, click on top right "Connect Wallet", choose Ethereum and WalletConnect. A QR Code would be displayed. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app, go to Settings -> WalletConnect. Use the camera to scan the QR code showing on the Web Dapp. It will connect your Trust Wallet to 1inch. This allows you to make smart contract transactions with 1inch.

You can now swap USDC to sUSD. It involves 2 transactions: approving the token and executing the swap. It will take a while for the transactions to settle. There is gas fee involved when executing any transactions in Ethereum. A confirmation popup will be shown once it is executed successfully.

Once it is completed, you can find it in your Trust Wallet. If it is not shown, tap on the top right icon in Wallet tab. Search for sUSD and enable it.

Step 3

Deposit stablecoin token to portfolio pool

For Crypto Large Cap TrendMax, our portfolio is deployed to dHedge decentralized asset management platform. To buy, deposit sUSD in the pool. 

dHedge provides Web Dapp that is WalletConnect compatible. You can connect Trust Wallet to it.

Open dHedge Web Dapp

Make sure the pool contract address you are interacting with is 0x175db26fa692d44cece9b0c3d55764acb7c71349.

Click on top right "Connect Wallet", choose WalletConnect. A QR Code would be displayed. Open your Trust Wallet mobile app, go to Settings -> WalletConnect. Use the camera to scan the QR code showing on the Web Dapp. It will connect your Trust Wallet to dHedge. This allows you to make smart contract transactions with dHedge. 

You can now deposit sUSD to the pool by clicking on "Invest". Follow the instructions there. It involves 2 transactions: approving the token and executing the deposit. It will take a while for the transactions to settle. There is gas fee involved when executing any transactions in Ethereum.If the gas price is too high, you may want to wait until it is low to deposit. Check the current gas price at Gas Now. A confirmation popup will be shown once it is executed successfully.

To check your investment portfolio, go to "My Investment" in the Web Dapp.
